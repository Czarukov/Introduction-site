Outgrown
Outgrown is a relaxing puzzle game focusing on growth of plants and the beauty of their life cycle. In outgrown players can place down plants on a grid and by doing so all other plants will age, showing their unique characteristics.
It is a game made as a part of my Master's Degree project and is currently finished.
The Arcade: A Stranger Things Experience
The Arcade is a recreation of a famous scene from the series Stranger Things known as "the trip to arcade" which had unsettling enough of a feeling to inspire this project.
It is an experience made as a part of a group project where I had the role of the gameplay programmer.
Infernal Clash
Infernal Clash is a two player versus game where you need quick reflexes in order to outpoint your enemy. You get 90 seconds to get more points than the enemy and claim the hellish crown.
It is a game made as a part of a group project where I had the opportunity to craft the gameplay and put it together with provided art
Deep Seacrets
Deep Seacrets is a game where the player is a blind but still capable soldier, alone on a deep sea submarine, tasked with transporting a top secret government package. Your only company being your commander over the communications system and the internal warning system. The mission is going smoothly until the alarm goes off.
The goal of the game is to follow the commanders orders, form a mental map of the submarine and try to survive
It is an experience made as a part of a group project where I had the role of the gameplay programmer.
Phrogchamp
FrogChamp is a game about climbing up a tree as a frog, the catch being ofcourse that every fall is a massive loss for progress. The game is short but challenging. The idea has been based on Jump King but with a bunch of twists.
It is a game made specifically for the Minijam 79 Game Jam. The development and designing was completed in the span of 48 hours. Almost everything has been made by me within that time period.
GloopRunner
GloopRunner is a game where YOU get to make your own levels that you have to speedrun.
It is a game made specifically for the GMTK Game Jam 2023. The development and designing was completed in the span of 48 hours. Almost everything has been made by me within that time period.
